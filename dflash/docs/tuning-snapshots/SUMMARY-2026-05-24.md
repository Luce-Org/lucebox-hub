## What the data argues for

1. **Per-case effort tier picker** would beat any global budget on
   this benchmark â€” the optimal is "low for overthinkers, max for
   underthinkers." Heuristic: probably suite-typed (AIME-small vs
   GPQA-complex) or even per-question via a quick estimator.
2. **Disabling thinking entirely is a strong baseline** for hard
   cases where the model overthinks into wrong answers. The bragi
   no-think 4/4 so far is striking given that *every* thinking
   budget failed these cases.
3. **Sampling-mode retries are useful** for cloud-style servers
   (vidar) where re-running can flip a result. Greedy (cpp-server)
   doesn't get this benefit but is reproducible.
